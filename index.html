ABOUT ME

In 2019, amidst the global pandemic that led to widespread layoffs, including my own, I made a bold decision to step out of my comfort zone. Instead of succumbing to the challenges, I seized the opportunity to acquire new skills through online learning, utilizing my computer and the internet while still holding on to my expertise in Sales and Marketing.


Over the past three years, my professional journey has involved traversing thousands of websites each month, exposing me to a myriad of designs and functions employed for various marketing strategies. Intrigued by the mechanics of websites, I was inspired to delve into the realm of web development. Driven by a desire to showcase my creativity, I decided to build my own website as a testament to my skills.


This venture into web development is not merely a personal project; it's a commitment to excellence. I am eager to leverage my skills as a Web Developer to deliver client satisfaction in every project undertaken. The passion that fuels my drive for excellence has me excited about the promising opportunities that await in the future.
